I received the B.S. degree in Integrated Circuit Design and Integration System from Harbin University of Science and Technology, Harbin, China, in 2011, the M.S. degree in Electronic and Communication Engineering from Guizhou University, Guiyang, China, in 2014, and the Ph.D. degree in Integrated Circuit Design with Fert Beijing Institute, Beihang University, China, in 2020.

From 2020, I joined the Academy of Military Science of the People's Liberation Army as a research assistant professor. My research interest includes hybrid CMOS/spintronic integrated circuits design, In-Memory Computing, Intelligent Microprocessors, and Ultra Large-Scale Integrated Circuits
